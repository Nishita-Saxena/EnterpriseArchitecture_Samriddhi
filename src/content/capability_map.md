# Capability Map

## Overview

The Samriddhi Capability Map provides a structured view of the business capabilities required to support housing society governance, incident management, compliance tracking, vendor management, resident engagement, and predictive maintenance.

The capability model acts as the bridge between business requirements and solution architecture. It identifies the functional areas that the platform must support and provides the foundation for application, data, integration, and technology architecture decisions.

## Core Capability Domains

### Governance & Compliance
- Compliance Management
- Regulatory Reporting
- Audit Support
- Policy Enforcement

### Incident Management
- Incident Registration
- Incident Tracking
- Incident Resolution
- Escalation Management

### Resident Services
- Resident Onboarding
- NCRID Management
- Communication Management
- Notification Services

### Vendor Management
- Vendor Discovery
- Vendor Assignment
- SLA Monitoring
- Vendor Performance Management

### Financial Operations
- Payment Processing
- Subscription Management
- Financial Reporting

### Intelligence & Analytics
- Predictive Maintenance
- Analytics & Reporting
- KPI Monitoring
- Decision Support

### IoT Operations
- Sensor Management
- Device Monitoring
- Anomaly Detection
- Edge Processing

### Platform Administration
- Identity & Access Management
- Security Administration
- Configuration Management
- Platform Monitoring

## Strategic Outcome

The capability map ensures that all architecture domains remain aligned to business objectives and provides a common reference model for future platform expansion.
