# Success Metrics

The following metrics are used to evaluate programme performance across operational effectiveness, governance, compliance, integration, scalability, and business outcomes.

| ID    | Metric                                 | Baseline                                | Target                                                 | Success Criteria                |
| ----- | -------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------- |
| SM-01 | Incident Resolution Improvement        | Current resolution process unstructured | 40% faster resolution                                  | ≥ 40% improvement               |
| SM-02 | Insurance Claim Rejection Reduction    | 43% rejection rate                      | < 10% rejection rate                                   | < 10%                           |
| SM-03 | Operational Expenditure Reduction      | Current expenditure levels              | 25% reduction                                          | ≥ 25% reduction                 |
| SM-04 | Resident Transparency Satisfaction     | 22% satisfaction                        | 85% satisfaction                                       | ≥ 85%                           |
| SM-05 | Governance Decision Documentation Rate | No structured documentation             | Fully documented governance decisions                  | 100%                            |
| SM-06 | Audit Readiness Rate                   | Limited audit readiness                 | 95% audit readiness                                    | ≥ 95%                           |
| SM-07 | Evidence Availability Rate             | Incomplete evidence management          | Near-complete evidence availability                    | ≥ 95%                           |
| SM-08 | Ecosystem Integration Coverage         | No external integrations                | Municipal, insurance, and vendor ecosystem integration | 100% target coverage            |
| SM-09 | Vendor Marketplace Coverage            | No marketplace                          | 1,200+ vendors onboarded                               | 100% target achievement         |
| SM-10 | NCRID Implementation Coverage          | No federated identity framework         | NCRID operational across platform                      | 100% user coverage              |
| SM-11 | Society Onboarding Scale               | 0 societies                             | 250 societies onboarded                                | 100% pilot completion           |
| SM-12 | Economic Loss Reduction                | ₹8,500 crore annual losses              | Measurable reduction                                   | Positive year-on-year reduction |

---

## Measurement Approach

Metrics are monitored through operational dashboards, governance reports, audit reviews, and programme performance assessments. Measurements are collected throughout implementation and evaluated against target outcomes defined within the Architecture Vision and Implementation Roadmap.

---

## Traceability

The success metrics directly support the programme objectives of:

* Faster incident resolution
* Reduced insurance claim rejections
* Lower operational expenditure
* Improved governance transparency
* Increased audit readiness
* Stronger ecosystem integration
* Federated identity adoption
* Scalable platform expansion
* Reduction of economic losses across housing societies
