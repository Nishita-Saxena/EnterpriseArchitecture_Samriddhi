1. Operational Effectiveness
SM-01: Incident Resolution Improvement

Baseline: Current incident resolution process is unstructured and unmanaged.

Target: 40% faster incident resolution.

Calculation:

Incident Resolution Improvement (%) =

((Baseline Resolution Time − Current Resolution Time) ÷ Baseline Resolution Time) × 100

Success Criteria:

≥ 40% improvement.

Traceability:

Directly linked to the stated objective:
"Achieve 40% faster incident resolution."

2. Financial Impact
SM-02: Insurance Claim Rejection Reduction

Baseline: 43% rejection rate.

Target: Less than 10%.

Calculation:

Insurance Claim Rejection Rate (%) =

(Rejected Claims ÷ Total Claims Submitted) × 100

Success Criteria:

< 10%

Traceability:

Directly linked to:

43% claim rejection rate
Missing evidence and audit records
